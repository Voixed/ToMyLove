Hello my love, Bomi. This whole site is dedicated to you, and to how much I love you. I'm not good at saying these things out loud, and I'm sorry for that, so I'm saying them here instead, where I have the time to get them right.

I know this relationship probably looks rushed from the outside. It's long distance, it's only been about a week since I started writing this, and it's my first relationship and you already know that. But I try my hardest, every single day, just for you.

I know we both carry some doubts. I have mine too, and I choose to ignore them because being with you, and knowing you love me, is already enough for me. You're in my thoughts constantly and I hope it's the same for you. I've caught myself imagining our future more times than I can count, and picturing the moment we finally meet in person. I think about what I could do for you more than I probably let on.

I love listening to you talk. The way you open up about your culture, the things that fascinate you, even your insecurities. You are also the most beautiful and stunning person I have ever came across, and I mean that as plainly and as honestly as I can put it.

Whatever problems or hardships come our way, I would want to go through it with you every step of the way. You are the one and only I truly love deeply. And even if things somehow don't work out, which I hope with everything in me they will, I will cherish every single moment we spent together.

Always yours, Angelo
